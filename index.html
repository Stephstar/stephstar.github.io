About Me

Growing up in Northern Uganda, I saw how limited healthcare access affects entire communities — and that reality set my path. From Mbarara University of Science and Technology, I'm building technologies that bridge the gap between medical innovation and the communities that need them most.

As a Biomedical Engineering student, I work across medical imaging, AI, AR/VR for education, and telemedicine — always asking: How can technology make quality healthcare accessible to everyone, regardless of where they live?

"Building technologies that improve healthcare access and outcomes in underserved communities — starting with Uganda, reaching the world."

Projects

VR for BME Education

A virtual reality platform designed to enhance biomedical engineering education through immersive 3D simulations. The project aims to make complex anatomical and physiological concepts accessible through interactive VR experiences — especially valuable in resource-constrained learning environments.

Unity C# XR Interaction Toolkit 3D Modeling
Problem: Limited access to cadavers and expensive lab equipment for anatomy education.
Impact: Making BME education more engaging and accessible in Ugandan universities.
Lesson: User testing is crucial; VR in education requires careful pedagogical design.

Telehealth Pro

A comprehensive telemedicine web application built to address healthcare access challenges in underserved communities. Features secure video consultations, electronic health records management, and an intuitive interface designed for users with varying digital literacy levels.

React Node.js WebRTC PostgreSQL
Problem: Rural patients travel long distances for basic consultations.
Impact: Bridges the gap between rural patients and healthcare providers.
Lesson: Offline-first considerations are critical where internet connectivity is unreliable.

ProjectFlow

A project supervision and management platform designed to streamline MSc and undergraduate research workflows. The system helps students organize milestones, track progress, manage documentation, and maintain effective communication with supervisors through a centralized digital workspace.

React Firebase Node.js Project Management
Problem: Many student research projects suffer from poor organization, missed deadlines, and limited visibility of progress for supervisors.
Impact: Improves project tracking, communication, and accountability between students and supervisors while reducing administrative overhead.
Lesson: Successful project management tools must balance powerful functionality with simplicity, ensuring users can focus on research rather than learning complex software.
